Social media, What’s the big deal?

Well of the eight billion people that live on this earth, 83.36% of 4.57 billion global internet users and 90.71% of 4.20 billion mobile internet users are on social media. That's a lot of consumers looking to be entertained, informed and engaged. As a modern day business, it is your responsibility to supply this demand. If not, your competitors will be.

Why do I need a Social media Marketer

On the contrary to what most people think, there is so much more to social media than mere likes & shares. The real power lies in its capacity to offer a bigger, more focused reach to your business!

We will aid you in increasing and organically targeting your audience, ensuring that more potential customers see more of your amazing content and offers.

Keeping Customers Engaged

High quality content and consistency across all platforms is crucial when managing your social media platforms. It is just not possible to underestimate the significance of keeping your audience engaged with your brand, if you don't, be assured that your rivals will be.

Whenever it comes to your social media strategy, it doesn't matter what size your company is, you ought to think big! Building a loyal and committed audience ensures that your call to action posts, such as promotional offers and new product launches, receive a much better response.

Once we have spent time getting to know you and your business, our Newcastle-based social media team will develop a comprehensive marketing campaign consisting of eye-catching visuals and effective call to actions.

how does it work?

We recognise that the amount of work behind managing multiple social media pages on top of managing your business can be overwhelming, but we're here to help. For your FREE social media audit, contact our team.

At Anomalous Content and Design, we don't use a one size fits all approach. We understand that every business is as different than the people behind them. This is why our first move is to do a full social media analysis. This way we can see what is/ not working.

We will look at aspects such as whether you are using the most appropriate social media networks for your product or service, that you are consistent and on-brand, and how you are using any social media management tools that are available.

Once our team has finished stalking your business’ digital profile, we can then deliver your performance report and future plan for reaching your digital aims and objectives.

Don't worry, your plan will consider if it will be you managing your platforms or whether you need us to take care of that for you. No matter what field you work in, social media is a must, you can't underestimate the value of exposure and reputation online.
