Many people believe when it comes to branding a business, the logo is top dog. This is not true. Though your logo is important, it is just one aspect of your brand’s identity.

Branding is the art of creating an instantly recognisable aesthetic that evokes emotion, therefore it needs to be present in everything that you do. Branding is a key part of all strategic marketing campaigns and is used to build long-lasting impressions. We are here to help you achieve this.

Good Question! When developing a brand it is important that every aspect of your digital aesthetic is consistent with your brand's message.

My message? This is the core value that your brand represents. When someone thinks of your business, what is it that you want them to think of? Is it that you are strong and reliable? Creating A Full Brand Identity .

So now you know the basics of why branding is so important, let's talk about how we can help you create your business’ brand Identity. A Brand Identity has four main characteristics:

  • A logo
  • Colour Palette
  • Font
  • Tone of Voice

We build brand identities by first diving deep into the intricate details of your business. What makes it unique? This can be as simple as the quirky office building, to something more elaborate, like the different style in which you approach your target market to the rest of the industry.

While you ponder this, ask yourself some easier questions. This is the stuff that defines your brand, making it more attractive. What does your business offer? What are its key values? Who is the desired customer? - Though all fairly simple questions, it is worth spending time really thinking about the right answer.
