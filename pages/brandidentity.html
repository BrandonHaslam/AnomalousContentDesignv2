First of all we will get to know you and your business a lot better, learning what it is you offer, who your target audience is and what you need from a website inorder to meet your desired call to action.

You will work closely with your dedicated account manager who will be able to assess your current digital marketing strategy and see if it needs tweeking or needs a complete rebuild.

It may be more cost and time effective to start from scratch.

We will then go away and work on your new website design. If you are happy with what our team has produced then we move forward with the development of your site. Our website developers use the latest technology to ensure you end up with a clean, responsive site that fits its purpose.

We design our websites mobile first. The reason being that over half of all ecommerce in the UK is expected to come from mobile by 2021. The importance of mobile websites is more than just online transactions. It is common practice for consumers to inspect a business, product or service using their mobile before or while using a physical retailer. It is now not good enough to be mobile responsive, your website now needs to be just as slick and vibrant on mobile and tablet devices as they are on desktop.

We design all our websites mobile first, by doing so you can be assured that your website looks amazing on all your customers devices.
